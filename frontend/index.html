Key Signals

Understanding the prediction signals.

These behavioral metrics are the strongest predictors of purchase intent in an online shopping session.

💰

Page Values

Average value of pages visited before completing a transaction. The single strongest predictor — higher values indicate strong purchase intent.

High Impact
🛍️

Product Related

Number of product pages visited during the session. More product views directly correlate with stronger buying intention signals.

Behavioral
↩️

Bounce Rates

Percentage of visitors entering and leaving from the same page. Lower bounce rates suggest deeper engagement and higher conversion potential.

Engagement
🚪

Exit Rates

Percentage of page sessions ending there. High exit rates on product pages are a strong signal of potential abandonment.

Conversion
